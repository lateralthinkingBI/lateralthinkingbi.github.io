Lateral Thinking BI nació de la convicción de que las mejores decisiones de negocio se toman con información ordenada y bien presentada. Combinamos formación contable, experiencia en grandes organizaciones y conocimiento técnico en Business Intelligence.

Lateral Thinking BI was born from the conviction that the best business decisions are made with organized and well-presented information. We combine accounting expertise, experience in major organizations and technical knowledge in Business Intelligence.

We have a track record in leading companies across different industries, giving us an approach that goes beyond the tool: we understand the business behind the numbers.

Llevamos ese conocimiento a organizaciones que quieren contar con las mismas herramientas analíticas que las grandes corporaciones, de manera accesible y práctica.

We bring that knowledge to organizations that want access to the same analytical tools as large corporations, in an accessible and practical way.
